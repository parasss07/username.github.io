WELCOME TO MY ART PORTFOLIO

About Me

       Hello everyone, I am Ojaswinee, a grade 10 student and this is my art portfolio. I have been testing my artistic abilities since a young age. My first few sketches were back in grade 3 which were off-looking drawings of Disney princesses. From there, I started trying to draw cartoon characters and and basic graphic art. Skip to 2 years later, I started to learn more about realism and colour theory. I started working with paints, oil pastels, colour pencils, clay, and textures on canvas.

       Now, I mostly work with acryclic paints, HB lead pencils, and I have my days with air-dry clay. I try to improve my skills independently through content online, joining clubs at school and by looking up inspirations and tips. Painting on big canvases was a huge step but also an important one as giving life to a canvas half your size gives you a feeling of accomplishment. Finally, to push myself and progress even more I have decided to display my projects in this online art portolio. I hope you find my work interesting!!
